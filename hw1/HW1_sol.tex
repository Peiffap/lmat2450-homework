	We define, for $s_1,s_2\in\{0,1\}^n$, $G'(s_1||s_2):=G(s_1)||s_2$ where $G'(s_1||s_2)\in \{0,1\}^{l(n)+n}$.
	
	Show that either $G'$ is a PRG by offering a reduction to the
        security of $G$, or that $G'$ is not a PRG by exhibiting an
